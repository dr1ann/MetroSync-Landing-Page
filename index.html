ABOUT

  • All Inclusive Tracking

    Covers the entire spectrum of transportation within the city, ensuring a seamless tracking experience for users across different modes of transport.

  • Schedule Management

    Facilitates real-time tracking of schedules for each mode of transportation, allowing commuters to plan their journeys more effectively.

  • Efficient Waiting Places

    Identifies and recommends appropriate waiting places for each transportation mode, minimizing confusion and streamlining the boarding process.

  • Uber and Grab-like Booking

    : In addition to tracking, the platform provides a convenient booking system for ships and rides, akin to the popular models of Uber and Grab.

  • Fair Fare Tracking

    Keeps users informed about minimum fares, as well as discounts for students, persons with disabilities (PWD), and seniors.
